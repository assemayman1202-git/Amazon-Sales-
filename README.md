# Amazon-Sales-Dataset
Data source

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohiteng/amazon-sales-dataset/code

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Amazon e-commerce sales from 2020 to 2024, focusing on revenue performance, customer behavior, product categories, brands, and operational efficiency. It provides an interactive view to help stakeholders understand sales trends, customer value, and business performance across time and regions.

Key KPIs include total revenue of $91.02M, total sales amount of $91.83M, 100,000 total customers, 43,233 unique customers, and an average revenue per customer of $918.26. Additional financial metrics include $6.85M in taxes, $740.67K in shipping costs, and applied discounts across orders.

The dashboard features interactive filters for year, month, category, brand, and payment method, allowing users to drill down into specific segments and time periods for deeper analysis.

Visualizations highlight total sales by category and brand, top products by revenue, quantity, and average price, as well as customer-focused insights such as new versus repeat customers, retention rate trends, and top customers by revenue.

Operational insights include delivery status distribution, payment method preferences, revenue by U.S. state, growth rate by year, and sales patterns by day and month.

Key insights show Electronics as the highest revenue category, strong performance from leading brands such as Apex and UrbanStyle, a high share of repeat customers (61.36%), improving customer retention reaching over 80% in 2024, dominant use of card-based payments, and efficient order fulfillment with most orders successfully delivered.
